About

libremate is a free online bookshelf for readers that would like a clean, clear place to keep track of their collection and to keep their thoughts about the books they love (or don't!) We like to showcase community reviews to get a feel for what your book-loving friends are reading, but libremate can be kept completely private as well if you'd like to keep your books and your thoughts to yourself. We believe there is something deeply precious about keeping a record of the way a book has touched your heart and mind; here on libremate you may record your thoughts and feelings about a book and rest assured they will be kept safe for you to revisit when time has passed and calls for reflection.
